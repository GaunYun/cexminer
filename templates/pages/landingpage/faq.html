Is it safe?

We highly recommend you to switch off API withdrawal rights, so in the unlikely event of a data breach, it is still impossible for hackers to access your funds. Moreover, the exchanges we work with have a strong reputation for maintaining strict safety standards.

Are profits guaranteed?

No, they’re not. The crypto market has remained significantly volatile. That being said, if you Invest in currencies you trust, conduct your own research and discover your winning strategies, you can become the master of your own success.

Are the prices refundable?

No, they aren’t. Try before you buy with our free trial, and then pay per month or buy for a whole year when you think you’re ready to take your trades to the next level.

Can I get rich by using this bot?

We spend a lot of time and effort to build this tool for you, like a hammer. Some people can build a house with it. Some might not and hurt themself.
